Project

To create an environmentally friendly packaging solution for an existing product that is not currently.

Research

When researching candy packages many used plastic bags. I decided to look into Maynards candy.

My solution was to create reusable or recycleable containers to place the candy in. With this idea, I thought creating a party pack with a bunch of flavours would be beneficial.
